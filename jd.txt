**Job Title:** Junior Python Developer - Sparkle Squad

**Company:** RainbowSparkles

**Location:** Remote (with occasional travel to Austin, TX)

**About RainbowSparkles:**

At RainbowSparkles, we believe in bringing joy and innovation to the world of personalized learning experiences. We’re a small, passionate team dedicated to building beautiful, impactful 
platforms that make learning more engaging and accessible. We value creativity, collaboration, and a good dose of sparkle! We’re looking for someone who’s eager to learn, grow, and 
contribute their skills to our vibrant team.

**Job Summary:**

As a Junior Python Developer on the Sparkle Squad, you’ll be working alongside experienced developers to build, maintain, and enhance our core learning platform and systems. You'll learn 
by doing, contributing to exciting projects, and helping us create even more rainbow-bright solutions. This is an amazing opportunity to kickstart your Python development career and grow 
your skills in a supportive and fun environment.

**Responsibilities:**

* **Code Development:** Write clean, well-documented, and testable Python code.
* **Learning & Growth:** Work closely with senior developers to learn best practices, new technologies, and our development processes.
* **Bug Fixing & Troubleshooting:** Assist in identifying, investigating, and resolving software bugs and issues.
* **Testing:** Participate in writing and executing unit tests and integration tests.
* **Collaboration:** Work effectively within a team, contributing ideas and participating in code reviews.
* **Documentation:** Help maintain and update technical documentation.
* **Small Project Contributions:** Take ownership of small, well-defined development tasks.
* **Embrace the Sparkle:** Contribute to a positive and collaborative team environment!

**Qualifications:**

* **Education:** Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field (or equivalent experience).
* **Programming Fundamentals:** Solid understanding of programming concepts, including data structures and algorithms.
* **Python Experience:** Familiarity with Python programming language and its core libraries (e.g., NumPy, Pandas, Requests).
* **Version Control:** Experience with Git and GitHub.
* **Problem-Solving Skills:** A keen eye for detail and a desire to solve challenging problems.
* **Bonus Points:** Experience with Django, familiarity with PostgreSQL.

**What We Offer:**

* **Salary:** $65,000 - $85,000 per year (commensurate with experience)
* Flexible Work Environment (Remote-first)
* Opportunities for professional development and training.
* A fun, collaborative, and supportive team culture.
* Company Swag (including rainbow-themed hoodies!)

**To Apply:**

Please submit your resume and a brief cover letter (telling us why you’d love to join the RainbowSparkles team!) to jobs@rainbowsparkles.com
